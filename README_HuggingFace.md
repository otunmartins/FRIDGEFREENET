# 🩹 Insulin AI - Fridge-Free Delivery Patches

An AI-powered research assistant for discovering and optimizing materials for insulin delivery patches that don't require refrigeration. This application combines artificial intelligence with materials science to accelerate research into novel pharmaceutical delivery systems.

## 🚀 Features

### 🤖 Multi-Mode AI Assistant
- **General Chat**: Ask questions about insulin delivery and pharmaceutical research
- **Literature Mining**: Search and analyze scientific papers for relevant insights
- **PSMILES Generator**: Create and validate polymer structures for material design
- **Research Mode**: In-depth technical discussions and methodology guidance

### 🔬 Research Capabilities
- Insulin stability analysis and formulation strategies
- Transdermal delivery mechanism exploration
- Polymer material selection and design
- Literature search and synthesis
- Chemical structure generation and validation

### 🧪 Specialized Features
- **PSMILES (Polymer SMILES)**: Generate specialized polymer notation for materials research
- **Material Discovery**: Identify promising compounds and formulations
- **Property Prediction**: Analyze material characteristics for insulin delivery
- **Research Guidance**: Methodology suggestions and experimental design
